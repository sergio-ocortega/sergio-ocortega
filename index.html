About Me

My name is Sergio

I’m 27 years old and I’m from Chile, currently trying to make a living by cleaning in Berlin and pay for my studies (Full-Stack Web Development). I only started working in this around 5 months ago, in which I’ve had my fair share of experience cleaning around X apartments, some clubs, offices and huge venues. Back home I worked in many things, mostly linked to media: camera-man and sound-mixer in documentaries/short films, some graphic design / video editing, etc. But most of my experience comes from around 9 years working at a Cultural Center / Archive, in which I was in charge of the digitizing and managing of old photos, films and newspapers from ca1900 - ca2000 and helping putting together exhibitions for the public to see them.
